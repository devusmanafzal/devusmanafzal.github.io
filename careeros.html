Questions

Good to know.

Who is this program for?

Students and early professionals who want a practical system for growth anyone unsure where to start, how to stay consistent, or how to communicate their value in an AI-driven world.

How much time does it take each week?

Each week pairs a short instructor-led session with focused, hands-on work that produces one tangible output. The steps are small and designed to fit around studies or a job.

Is this just "ChatGPT 101"?

No. AI fluency is one of five pillars. You'll learn prompting and verification, then apply AI inside real workflows you build and keep, not isolated tips.

Can it run for a whole class or cohort?

Yes. Career OS is built to run as an institutional cohort, individually or in teams. We provide the curriculum, materials, and facilitation.

What do participants actually walk away with?

Nine real outputs including a North Star Document, Personal Brand One-Pager, Prompt Playbook, Capstone Project, and a Career OS Playbook with a 90-day action plan.
